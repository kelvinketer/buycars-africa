Corporate Governance

Anti-Bribery & Corruption (ABC) Policy

1. Statement of Intent

BuyCars.Africa maintains a zero-tolerance stance toward all forms of bribery and corruption. We are committed to acting professionally, fairly, and with integrity in all our business dealings and relationships, regardless of the jurisdiction in which we operate.

2. Core Prohibitions

Our employees, partners, and merchants are strictly prohibited from:

  • Offering, promising, or giving a bribe or kickback to influence a business outcome.
  • Requesting, agreeing to receive, or accepting a financial or other advantage as an inducement for improper performance.
  • Engaging in any activity that creates a conflict of interest or undermines the transparency of our marketplace.

3. Standards of Compliance

To ensure institutional credibility, we align our internal systems with ISO 37001 (Anti-Bribery Management Systems). Our financial transactions are processed under PCI DSS standards, and our operational data is secured under ISO 27001 to prevent unauthorized manipulation of merchant records.

4. Whistleblowing & Reporting

We empower our community to act as the primary guardians of our integrity. Any suspicion of unethical behavior can be reported anonymously via our Whistleblowing Portal. Every report is investigated with institutional rigor, and whistleblowers are protected from any form of retaliation.

5. Merchant Accountability

Compliance with this ABC Policy is a mandatory requirement for all Platinum, Silver, and Emerald partners. Failure to adhere to these standards results in immediate suspension from the ecosystem and the forfeiture of "Verified Dealer" status.
