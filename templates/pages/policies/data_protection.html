Digital Integrity & Trust

Data Protection & Privacy Policy

1. Our Data Sovereignty Pledge

At BuyCars.Africa, we believe that your digital identity is your property. We act as custodians, not owners, of your information. We pledge to never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. Your trust is the foundation of our institution.

2. Legal Compliance Framework

Our data processing activities are strictly governed by the Data Protection Act, 2019 (Kenya) and aligned with the principles of the GDPR for our international users. We are registered as a Data Controller and Processor with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC).

Lawful Basis: We only collect data necessary for transaction verification, fraud prevention, and legal compliance (KYC).

3. Bank-Grade Security Standards

To ensure the highest level of protection, we employ institutional-grade security measures:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • ISO 27001 Alignment: Our information security management systems adhere to ISO 27001 global standards.
  • PCI DSS Compliance: All payment card transactions are processed in a PCI DSS compliant environment, ensuring your financial data never touches our servers directly.

4. How We Use Your Data

Data Category Purpose Retention Period
Identity Data (ID, Passport) Dealer Verification (KYC) & Fraud Prevention 7 Years (Legal Requirement)
Contact Data (Phone, Email) Transaction Updates & Booking Confirmations Duration of Active Account
Financial Data Processing Payments (Encrypted) Not Stored (Tokenized only)

5. Your Rights

You retain full control over your data. Under the law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate information instantly via your dashboard.
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request the deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention obligations.
